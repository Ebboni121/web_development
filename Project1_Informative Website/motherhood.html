Challenges to Expect After becoming a Mom...

Emotional challenges - Mothers may feel guilt if they worry they aren't doing a good enough job, or experience stress from the demands of parenting, work, and household chores. They may also feel isolated and lonely, especially if they are the primary caregiver and spend most of their time with their children.

Mental Exhaustion - Mothers may feel an unequal burden and expectation placed on them, and may be the subject of judgment when others when they decide to not take advice and do things thier way. They may also face challenges of lack of time for self-care, mom shaming, parenting comparison, teaching morals and values, maintaining discipline, and dealing with the educational system.

Physical demands - New mothers may experience exhaustion from adapting to a demanding sleep and feed schedule, and from the physical demands of breastfeeding. This can include pain when latching on, cracked nipples, and mastitis.
